Built for Hosts, by Hosts.

iCleaners was born from the frustration of managing short-term rentals in Nairobi. We know the stress of last-minute bookings, unexpected guest extensions, and the mad dash to clean a property between check-out and check-in. Standard cleaning companies just weren't fast enough.

So we built the solution we needed: an on-demand platform with a network of vetted, professional cleaners ready to be dispatched at a moment's notice. Our technology ensures the nearest cleaner is assigned to your property, guaranteeing the fastest possible turnaround time.

Our mission is simple: to help you maximize your occupancy, get better reviews, and run your Airbnb business stress-free.
